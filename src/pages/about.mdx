## About

Hi, I'm Rey, a software developer, autodidact, and \*nix aficionado. This is my homepage on the World Wide Web.  
  
I make full-stack web applications with the MERN stack, Django, PostgreSQL, and the [Jamstack](https://jamstack.org). Other technologies that I use on a regular/semi-regular basis can be found <Link to="/technologies">here</Link>. I am interested in [theoretical computer science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theoretical_computer_science), particularly machine learning and mathematics.

I was born around [978307200](https://duckduckgo.com/?q=unix+time+978307200) seconds after the Unix epoch. Being a [perfectionist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perfectionism_(psychology)) and an aspiring [polymath](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polymath), I like to share what I find interesting and learn everything I can.
